About Us

Welcome to Anna Infotech ! We are an MKCL (Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited) Authorized Learning Center dedicated to providing high-quality education and training to help you excel in the digital world. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in today’s fast-paced and ever-evolving technological landscape.

Our Courses :

MS-CIT (Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology) :

The MS-CIT course is a comprehensive introduction to the world of computers and the internet. Designed for beginners and intermediate learners, this course covers essential topics such as:
* Basic computer operations
* Internet browsing and online communication
* Office automation tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
* Digital literacy and cybersecurity fundamentals

Programming Careers:
